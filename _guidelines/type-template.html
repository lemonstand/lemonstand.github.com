Type and whitespace

We are currently using the following typefaces:

  • Proxima Nova

    Used for smaller headlines and body text. Smart and functional.

  • Proxima Nova light

    Used for headlines and larger text. Elegant and lovely.

  • FF Tisa Pro

    Used to show the voice of the people. For quotes, notes, and descriptive elements written by our users and well wishers.

  • Montserrat

    Used for buttons and interactive controls. It's a bold, imperative to do something now. Especially powerful when combined with ALL-CAPS.
